\section{Soft Skills}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Problem-solving: Focus on identifying root causes rather than only symptoms, then selecting practical and sustainable solutions based on project constraints, timelines, and system impact.
  \item Presentation skills: Confident in delivering presentations in both English and Vietnamese, with careful preparation and clear, audience-friendly slides that explain problems, technical approaches, and proposed solutions.
  \item English communication: Fluent in daily communication with international teammates and comfortable discussing technical topics, with strong reading ability for technical references and academic IT documentation.
  \item Teamwork: Listen respectfully to team members, align on shared goals, communicate proactively when issues arise, and collaborate effectively with peers and seniors to resolve problems and improve outcomes.
\end{itemize}
